There's less than a month left until Christmas! When I stopped by the house, I was surprised to find it beautifully decorated. It really makes me happy when residents take the initiative to do this kind of thing on their own!

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_6368-1024x768.jpg)

Changing up the lighting made a difference too — pretty photogenic, if I do say so myself lol

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_6367-1-768x1024.jpg)

The birch-style tree got a stylish makeover as well.

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_6365-768x1024.jpg)

And there's a little tree on the dining table too.

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_6370-768x1024.jpg)

On the wall in front of the bathroom, there's now a handmade advent calendar!  
Pretty impressive, right? It was made by one of our British housemates!

Hope everyone has a wonderful Christmas season!  
And you young folks out there, go make some memories~! 😂
