The sofa and chairs in The Headquarters Kobe's living room have been there since the previous owner's days, and because of how they're built, we've never been able to wash them properly, so they'd gotten a bit dirty and worn-looking. So we decided to put new covers on the sofa. Ideally I'd love to build something custom that fits the whole space as one cohesive design, but for now this'll do as a stopgap 😅

Here's the before 👇

![Sofa before recovering it](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/ソファカバー-1-1024x683.jpeg)

And the after 👇

![Sofa after putting on the new cover](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/ソファカバー-3-1024x683.jpeg)

Glad to see the residents are happy with it 😊 so far sofa so good!! 😆
