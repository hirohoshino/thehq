入居者の神戸大大学院生K君が「トビタテ生」の仲間を連れてきてくれました。  
HQのリビングを使ってもらい、残りの学生生活をどう楽しむかについてディスカッションしたらしい。学生って羨ましいですね（笑）。  
HQには大きなスクリーンもあるので勉強会もできます（今プロジェクターは故障中だけど💦）。

K君は今年の３月にトビタテ生の集まりとして遊びに来てくれた際にHQを気に入ってくれて入居してくれてました。８月以降は卒業前に海外などあちこち行くらしく、実は今日で退去。残念だけど就職まで残りの期間おもいきり楽しんでね！  
  
さて８月・９月からはまた別の神戸大の院生さんも入るので、神戸の縁が繋がっていくと嬉しいですね

Our resident K, a grad student at Kobe University, brought along some of his fellow "Tobitate" scholarship students to visit.  
They used the HQ living room and apparently had a whole discussion about how to make the most of the rest of their student life. Being a student sounds pretty enviable, doesn't it (lol).  
HQ also has a big screen, so study sessions are possible too (though the projector is broken right now 💦).

K first fell in love with HQ back in March when he came to hang out with a group of Tobitate students, and he ended up moving in. From August onward he's off traveling abroad and elsewhere before graduation, so today is actually his move-out day. It's a bit sad, but we hope he makes the absolute most of the time he has left before starting work!

From August/September another Kobe University grad student will be moving in, so it's nice to see these Kobe connections keep carrying on.
