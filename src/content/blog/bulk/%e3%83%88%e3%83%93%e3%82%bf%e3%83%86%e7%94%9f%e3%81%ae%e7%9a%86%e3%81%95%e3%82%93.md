ヘッドクオーターズ神戸に、縁があって関西のトビタテ生の皆さんが遊びに来てくれました。  
トビタテ生とは、文部科学省が展開する「_トビタテ_！_留学_JAPAN」（＝日本の若者の海外留学への気運を醸成する官民協働の留学促進キャンペーン、だそう）の制度で留学を経験した学生さん・社会人のひとたちです。  
多額の支援金をもらえる人気のある制度に応募して採用されたみなさんなので、優秀なのはもちろんエネルギーと自立心に溢れたひとたちでした。みんな頑張ってね！

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/IMG_5589-1024x763.jpg)

---

Thanks to a nice connection, a group of "Tobitate" students from the Kansai area came to visit The Headquarters Kobe.  
"Tobitate" students are people who studied abroad through the program run by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology called "_Tobitate_! _Ryugaku_ JAPAN" (apparently a public-private campaign to build momentum for young Japanese people studying overseas).  
Since they all applied for and were accepted into this popular program that comes with a generous grant, every one of them was, of course, talented — but also full of energy and independence. Good luck to you all!
