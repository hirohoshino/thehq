世間では今週末がハロウィンで盛り上がるのかな。神戸港のあたりとかどうなるんでしょう。水道筋商店街ではハロウィンイベントを先週末にやることになっていたので、それに合わせてヘッドクオーターズでも一足早くハウス内パーティが行われました😊。  
  
住人の女性達が頑張って盛り上げてくれたようですし、お友達も呼んで楽しかったみたいですね。写真は女性だけですが、男性も後から参加したみたい。こういうイベントごとを住人同士で楽しめるのも、シェアハウスならではの楽しみですね。恥ずかしがりやのひとでも、着替えてから会場に着くまで気まずくないし（笑）  
  
壁とか、ちょっと絵面が寂しいので来年はもっと装飾を増やさないといけないですね😅。

I wonder if the whole city will be buzzing with Halloween this weekend. I'm curious what it'll be like down around Kobe Port. The Suidosuji shopping street had its Halloween event last weekend, so to match that, we got a jump on things and held our own house party at The Headquarters a little early 😊.

The women of the house worked hard to get everyone in the spirit, and it sounds like they had friends over and had a blast. The photos only show the women, but apparently the guys joined in later too. Being able to enjoy this kind of event together with your housemates is one of the perks that's unique to share house life. Even if you're the shy type, there's no awkwardness between getting changed into your costume and actually showing up to the party (lol).

The walls and decorations looked a bit sparse, so next year we'll definitely need to step it up 😅.
