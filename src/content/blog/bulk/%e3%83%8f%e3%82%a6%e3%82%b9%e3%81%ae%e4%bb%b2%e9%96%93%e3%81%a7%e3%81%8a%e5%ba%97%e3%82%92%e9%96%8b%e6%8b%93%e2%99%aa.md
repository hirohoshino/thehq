*🇬🇧 English translation follows the Japanese text below.*

ヘッドクォーターズは水道筋商店街の中にあるので、近くに飲食店もそれなりにあるものの、自炊するひとが多いし近すぎると意外と行く気になりにくいのか、あまり皆が知っているごはんのお店は少ないようです。  
８月以降に入居した新しい仲間も増えたので「お店を開拓しよう！」という声が住人からあがり、先日７人で一緒に行ってきたようです。  
ますます楽しいシェアハウス生活になってきてますね😊

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/35865-1024x768.jpg)

---

The Headquarters sits right in the middle of Suidosuji shopping street, so there are plenty of restaurants nearby. But since so many of us cook for ourselves, and maybe because it's almost too close to home, it turns out most of us haven't actually tried many of the local spots.  
With a bunch of new housemates having moved in since August, someone said "let's go explore some of the local restaurants!" and a group of seven headed out together the other day.  
Shared house life just keeps getting more fun 😊
