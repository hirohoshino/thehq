(the company behind the app, famous for their crowdfunding platform) interviewed us and wrote up a bit about what goes on inside our house.  
(Maybe they reached out to us because we're such active users relative to our size?)

I ended up rambling on about all sorts of things 😅, but the editor put together a nice write-up, so please check it out if you're interested.
