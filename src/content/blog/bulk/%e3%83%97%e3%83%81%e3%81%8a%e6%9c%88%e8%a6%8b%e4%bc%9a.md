日本の中秋の名月は明日ですが、台湾では月餅を食べるイベントが今日みたい。台湾人の住人がお団子を買ってきてそれを教えてくれて、何人かで屋上にあがって食べました。この季節、屋上があまりに気持ち良くて寝たくなります笑。

Tomorrow is Japan's mid-autumn harvest moon, but apparently in Taiwan today is the day for eating mooncakes. One of our Taiwanese housemates picked up some dango (rice dumplings) and told us all about the custom, so a few of us headed up to the rooftop to enjoy them together. This time of year the rooftop feels so good you just want to lie down and nap there, haha.
