先日の朝にシェアハウスメンバー2人で、近所のラジオ体操に参戦してきました。朝早く起きれない…という悩みを共有し、一緒にラジオ体操に行ってくれる仲間がいるのがシェアハウスの良いところ。

そのあとは2人で、ワンコインで豪華海鮮丼が食べられるという春日野道の「神東寿司」へ。噂通りのコスパの良さにびっくり。セットで出てくるあら汁は体に染み渡る美味しさでした。

投稿 by Y.

---

The other morning, two of our share house members headed out together to join the neighborhood's radio calisthenics (rajio taiso). "I just can't get up early enough..." is a struggle a lot of us share, and having housemates willing to drag themselves out of bed and go with you is one of the best things about share house life.

Afterward, the two of them stopped by Kanto Sushi (神東寿司) in Kasuganomichi, famous for serving a gorgeous seafood rice bowl for just one coin. It really did live up to its reputation for great value, and the fish broth that came with the set was so comforting it warmed them right through.
