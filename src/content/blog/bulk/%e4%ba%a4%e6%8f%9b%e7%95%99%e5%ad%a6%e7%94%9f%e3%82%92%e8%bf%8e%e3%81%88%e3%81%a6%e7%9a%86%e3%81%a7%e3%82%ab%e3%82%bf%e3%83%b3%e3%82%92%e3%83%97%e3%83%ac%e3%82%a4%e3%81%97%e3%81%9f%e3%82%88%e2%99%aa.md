新しくイギリスのマンチェスター大学から交換留学生の方をヘッドクォーターズに迎えました。これで8月・9月の入退去シーズンの予定はすべて終わり、また満室に戻りました。  
交換留学生の方は英国とハンガリーの二重国籍の方で、これから神戸大学で日本語と経営学を学ぶとのことです。まだ話せる日本語はほんの少しですが、英語をまじえて会話するのは皆にとってよい刺激になっていますね😊。  
歓迎会のプチ飲みから続いてカタンを始めたら皆で楽しくなり、気づいたら深夜3時ぐらいでした😅  
国際的で楽しいシェアハウスになってきています👍

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/hqkobebbq-1-1-1024x436.jpg)

---

We've welcomed a new exchange student from the University of Manchester in the UK to The Headquarters. With that, all our move-in/move-out plans for the August-September season are wrapped up, and we're back to a full house.  
Our new exchange student holds dual British and Hungarian citizenship, and will be studying Japanese and Business Management at Kobe University. Their Japanese is still just a handful of words, but mixing in English for conversations has been a great bit of stimulation for everyone 😊.  
What started as a little welcome drink turned into a game of Catan, and everyone was having so much fun that before we knew it, it was around 3am 😅  
The house is turning into a wonderfully international and fun share house 👍
