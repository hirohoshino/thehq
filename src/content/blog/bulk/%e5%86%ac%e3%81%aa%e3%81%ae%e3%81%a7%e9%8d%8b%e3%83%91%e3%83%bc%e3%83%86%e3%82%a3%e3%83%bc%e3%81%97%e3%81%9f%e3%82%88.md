だんだん寒くなってきたのと、新しい住人の留学生カップル（英国の大学に学籍があり神戸大に留学中のハンガリー人）に日本の伝統料理（笑）を楽しんでもらおうと鍋パをやりました！まだ白菜は安くなかったけどね。久しぶりに大人数で鍋！  
こういうのほんとシェアハウスの良さですね、みんなで楽しみました😊

鍋のあとは、最近ハウス内で機会が増えてるゲーム会も。この夜は新しく買ったドブルという反射神経系のゲームと、カップルがハンガリーから持ってきてくれたHanabiというゲームをやりました。

It's getting colder, and we also wanted our new resident couple (a Hungarian couple who are enrolled at a UK university but currently studying abroad at Kobe University) to enjoy some "traditional Japanese cuisine" (lol), so we threw a nabe hot pot party! Napa cabbage still wasn't cheap yet, but oh well. It had been a while since we did a big-group nabe night!  
This is really what's great about share house life — everyone had a blast together 😊

*   ![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/なべ-1-1024x768.jpg)
    
*   ![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/なべ-2-1024x682.jpg)
    
*   ![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/なべ-5-768x1024.jpg)
    

After the nabe, we moved on to game night, which has been happening more and more often in the house lately. That night we played Dobble, a new reflex-based game we just bought, along with Hanabi, which the couple brought over with them from Hungary.
