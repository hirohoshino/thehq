先週後半から雨が続きますね。  
ヘッドクオーターズ神戸の屋上工事も残念ながらストップしたまま💦。はやく夏の青空の下でバーベキューをやりたいものです😊。

さてそんな雨の時でも濡れずに買い物が出来るのがアーケード商店街の良いところ。ヘッドクオーターズは徒歩1秒で水道筋商店街のメインストリートに出られますよ（笑）。

今日の写真は商店街の中でもディープなエリアをご紹介。知らなかったら絶対来ない迷路の中にあるお魚屋さん。活きのいい品ばかり、海老が跳ねてました。魚介のバーベキューも良さそうですね😊✨

It's been raining since late last week.  
Unfortunately, the rooftop construction at The Headquarters Kobe is still on hold 💦. We really want to get out there and have a barbecue under a clear summer sky 😊.

But one nice thing about rainy days like this is that you can still shop without getting wet, thanks to the covered arcade of the shopping street. From The Headquarters, it takes literally one second on foot to step out onto the main street of Suidosuji shopping arcade (ha!).

Today's photo introduces one of the more "deep" corners of the shopping street — a fish shop tucked away in a maze of alleys you'd never stumble across unless you already knew it was there. Everything is super fresh, and the shrimp were still jumping around. Sounds like a seafood barbecue would be pretty great too 😊✨
