10月13日（日曜）、水道筋商店街では「生活応援祭」と銘打って抽選会が行われ、シェアハウスの住人も並びにいったようです。当たるとお買い物券がもらえて生活用品がいろいろ安くなるみたいですね。  
12時開始で1,600本もくじがあったらしいけど、12時半過ぎに行ったらもう配布終了だったとのこと。。。水道筋商店街を舐めちゃいけないですね😅。  
ローカルでも意外と盛り上がってる商店街。ヘッドクォーターズのシェアハウス生活が楽しい大きな理由です😊。

On Sunday, October 13th, Suidosuji Shopping Street held a lottery event called the "Life Support Festival," and it sounds like some of our share house residents went to line up for it. If you win, you get a shopping voucher, so it sounds like all sorts of everyday items end up cheaper.  
Apparently there were 1,600 tickets up for grabs starting at noon, but by the time they got there just after 12:30, they were already all gone... Never underestimate Suidosuji Shopping Street 😅.  
It's a local shopping street, but it's surprisingly lively. That's one of the big reasons life at The Headquarters share house is so much fun 😊.
