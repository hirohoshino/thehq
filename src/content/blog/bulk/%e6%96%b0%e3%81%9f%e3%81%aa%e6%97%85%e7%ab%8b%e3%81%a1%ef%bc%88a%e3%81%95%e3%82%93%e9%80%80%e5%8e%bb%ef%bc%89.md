アフリカにエアコンを売るベンチャーで働くAさんが約１年の入居期間を経て、退去になりました。またタンザニアに長期出張になるとのこと。これまでも何回か出張で行っていてその間はHQに部屋を残してくれていましたが、今回は改めて解約となりました。

まあでもまた半年ぐらいで日本に帰ってくるらしいので、みんなと遊ぶことになるでしょう笑。泳いでないと死ぬマグロのような女性なので（笑）、きっと次は新たな環境を見つけることが吉なんでしょう。退去は毎回寂しいですが、シェアハウスって良い意味でそういうもの、つまり新陳代謝のある環境であるべきかなとも思います😊

A works for a venture company that sells air conditioners in Africa, and after about a year living with us, she's moved out. She's heading off on a long-term assignment to Tanzania. She's actually gone there for work trips a few times before, and each time she kept her room here at HQ waiting for her, but this time she officially gave notice and checked out for good.

That said, apparently she'll be back in Japan in about six months, so I'm sure we'll all be hanging out again before long (lol). She's like a tuna that has to keep swimming or she'll die, so I guess finding a new environment is exactly what suits her. It's always a little sad to see someone move out, but I think that's part of what makes a share house great in a good way — it's an environment with a healthy turnover of people 😊
