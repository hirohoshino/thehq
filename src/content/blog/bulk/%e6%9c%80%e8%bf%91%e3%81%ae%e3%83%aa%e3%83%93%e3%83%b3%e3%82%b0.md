This year at The Headquarters Kobe, we switched from being a short-stay hotel-style place to focusing purely on shared housing, so we've been changing up the layout of the living room (making it more open so a bigger group can use it comfortably).

The old, stylish vibe was really nice too, but these days we're more in "dinner party" mode, I guess (lol). This is where all the residents gather to eat together, watch movies, and just hang out — friends of residents come by too!
