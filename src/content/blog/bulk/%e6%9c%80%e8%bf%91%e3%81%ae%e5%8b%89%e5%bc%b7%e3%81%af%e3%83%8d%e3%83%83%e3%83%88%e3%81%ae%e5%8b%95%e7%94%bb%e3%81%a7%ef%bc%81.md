先日DIYで改装した勉強部屋ですが、さっそく住人も使ってくれてるようです。コロナで在宅勤務が増えたり、大学や高校もオンライン授業が増えてるので、今後もリモートワーク/スタディを前提にした住環境が必要になるんじゃないでしょうか。シェアハウスも共有部の充実さが価値なので、考えていかないといかないですね。

写真は勉強中のH君です。入居時はなんと中３でしたが今や高３、受験生です。時間経つのはやっ！！

何を勉強しているのかと見てみると・・・

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/IMG_1766-1024x768.jpg)

英語のようです。しかもユーチューブで（笑）。  
聞くと、「めっちゃわかりやすい」だそうです。まあそうですよね、各学校の普通の先生よりも、鍛え抜かれた予備校講師のほうが授業は面白くてわかりやすいはず。がんばれー！！

ところでヘッドクオーターズ神戸は３フロアあってうち２フロアが居室メインで（洗面ランドリールームとトイレあり）、１つのフロアがすべて共有部（スタディ、キッチン、ダイニング、リビング、トイレ、シャワー、バス）になっています。廊下も入れれば共有部が全体の半分以上ってかなり大きな割合なんじゃないかなと思います。

The study room we recently renovated ourselves (DIY-style) is already getting good use from our residents. With more people working from home because of COVID, and more universities and high schools moving classes online, it feels like living spaces built around remote work and remote study are only going to matter more going forward. Since shared spaces are such a big part of what makes a share house valuable, it's definitely something we need to keep thinking about.

The photo below shows H, hard at work studying. He moved in when he was in his third year of junior high, and now he's already a third-year high schooler gearing up for exams. Time really flies!

Curious what he was studying, we took a look...

![Photo of a resident studying at a desk](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/IMG_1766-1024x768.jpg)

Looks like English. And on YouTube, of all places (lol).  
When we asked him about it, he said it was "super easy to understand." Makes sense, really — a seasoned cram school instructor is bound to teach more engagingly and clearly than the average school teacher. Keep it up!!

By the way, The Headquarters Kobe has three floors — two are mainly private rooms (each with a washroom/laundry area and toilet), and the entire third floor is shared space (study room, kitchen, dining area, living room, toilet, shower, and bath). If you count the hallways too, shared space makes up more than half of the whole building, which is honestly a pretty huge proportion.
