*🇬🇧 English translation follows the Japanese text below.*

なんだか賑やかだなと外に出てみたら、商店街でお祭りをやっていました。メインストリートから枝道までたくさんの出店があり、老若男女のお客さんたちで賑わっていました。子供も多かったですね。  
地元のお祭りという感じでとてもよかったです😊

![](https://theheadquarters.jp/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/IMG_8936-1024x768.jpg)

It seemed unusually lively outside, so I went out to take a look — turns out the local shopping street was having a festival! There were tons of stalls, from the main street all the way down the side alleys, packed with people of all ages. Lots of kids too.  
It had that great, down-to-earth neighborhood festival vibe 😊
