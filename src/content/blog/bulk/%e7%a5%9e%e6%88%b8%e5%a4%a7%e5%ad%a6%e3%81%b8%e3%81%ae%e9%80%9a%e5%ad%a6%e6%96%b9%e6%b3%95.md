シェアハウスの入居希望者から神戸大への通学方法はどのようになるかをたまに聞かれるので、下記にメモをアップしておきます。

ちなみにヘッドクォーターズ（HQ）神戸は神戸大学と三宮の中間ほどに立地しています。このシェアハウスからは大学に行くにも三宮に遊びにいくにも、駅（JR摩耶駅 or 阪急王子公園駅）やバス停に近くて便利だし、水道筋商店街自体が暮らすうえで最高に便利です。  
そのため神戸大学関係者からけっこう人気がありますね😊。現在およびこれまでに何人か神戸大関係者が住んでくれてます。  
  
【通学方法】  
①阪急電車＋市バス：片道370円  
ハウスから歩7分→阪急王子公園駅→阪急六甲駅→バス36系統でバス停「神大正門前」  
  
②JR電車＋市バス：片道340円  
ハウスから歩6分→JR摩耶駅→JR六甲道駅→バス36系統でバス停「神大正門前」  
  
③市バス乗り継ぎ：片道210円  
ハウスから歩1分→92系統バス停「水道筋３丁目」→バス停「六甲口」で降車、36系統乗り換え→バス停「神大正門前」  
  
④自転車／原付で移動：  
自転車でもまあ行ける距離です。原付ならすぐ。  
  
⑤徒歩：  
大学からハウスへ帰り道は散歩がてら30分弱ぐらいかな？行きは登り（苦笑）。  
  
学生に聞いたらおすすめは③だそうですね。  
神戸市バスの乗り換えはICカードで乗れば2区間目が無料なので、210円で大学まで行けます（電車＋バスだと阪急で370円、JRで340円）。  
ハウスの最寄りのバス停は92系統の「水道筋３丁目」ですぐ近くだし、本数はだいたい１時間に７本以上は走ってます。  
  
乗り換えるバスは36系統で阪急六甲駅から混むようですが、バス乗り換えだとその前の「六甲口」バス停から一足はやく乗るので座れる確率が高いそうです（雨の日の朝だと阪急六甲からバスに乗れないことすらあるらしい）。ま、若者はできるだけ立って席を譲るべきですけど笑。

大学からの帰りは、天気と気分によっては徒歩の時もあるようですね。帰りは坂を下るのでだいぶ楽なようで。

We sometimes get asked by people interested in moving into the share house how the commute to Kobe University actually works, so here's a quick note on it.

By the way, The Headquarters (HQ) Kobe sits roughly halfway between Kobe University and Sannomiya. From the house it's easy to get to either the university or out for fun in Sannomiya, since we're close to both a train station (JR Maya or Hankyu Oji Koen) and bus stops — and the Suidosuji shopping street itself is just about the most convenient place you could ask for to live.  
That's probably why the house is pretty popular with Kobe University folks 😊. We've had — and currently have — several Kobe University students and staff living here.  

**【How to get to campus】**  
① Hankyu train + city bus: 370 yen one way  
7 min walk from the house → Hankyu Oji Koen Station → Hankyu Rokko Station → City bus route 36 to "Shindai Seimon-mae" stop  

② JR train + city bus: 340 yen one way  
6 min walk from the house → JR Maya Station → JR Rokkomichi Station → City bus route 36 to "Shindai Seimon-mae" stop  

③ City bus transfer only: 210 yen one way  
1 min walk from the house → Route 92 bus at "Suidosuji 3-chome" stop → get off at "Rokko-guchi," transfer to route 36 → "Shindai Seimon-mae" stop  

④ By bicycle or moped:  
It's a reasonable distance to cover by bike. On a moped, you're there in no time.  

⑤ On foot:  
Walking home from the university takes maybe just under 30 minutes if you treat it as a stroll. The way there is uphill though (ha).  

According to the students, option ③ is the one they recommend.  
If you tap an IC card for the Kobe city bus transfer, the second leg is free, so you can get to campus for just 210 yen (compared to 370 yen via Hankyu or 340 yen via JR with train + bus).  
The nearest bus stop to the house is "Suidosuji 3-chome" on route 92, and it's right around the corner — buses run at least seven times an hour or so.  

The connecting route 36 bus tends to get crowded once it picks people up at Hankyu Rokko Station, but if you transfer buses you catch it one stop earlier at "Rokko-guchi," so your odds of getting a seat are apparently much better (on rainy mornings, they say it can even get so packed you can't board at Hankyu Rokko at all). That said, young folks should stand and offer their seats whenever they can, ha.

👆 These are the two bus stops used for the transfer. They're both called "Rokko-guchi," but since they're on different routes, you'll walk about 40 meters between them.  

On the way back from campus, depending on the weather and how they're feeling, some people apparently walk home instead. It's mostly downhill on the return, so it's said to be a lot easier going.
