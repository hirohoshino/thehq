And then, a takoyaki party (lol). The takoyaki maker we have at the house runs on a portable gas canister, so it cooks the takoyaki up nice and crispy on the outside. We used to have an electric one, but it took forever to cook and left the takoyaki kind of soggy, not so tasty. So we switched to gas — and once you go gas, there's no going back. Seriously, gas is the way to go. The Headquarters is a share house with great takoyaki too (lol).
