The Headquarters Kobe boasts generous and well-equipped shared spaces. Nearly one-third of the total floor area — or close to half including the rooftop — is common space. Water and bathroom facilities are plentiful relative to the number of residents.
The building itself is older, but the common areas are cleaned by a housekeeper once a week, and residents tend to keep things tidy, so you'll find it very comfortable to live here.

## Living Space

The 5th floor is primarily shared space, featuring a spacious living room where residents relax and share meals together.
There's also a TV and gaming consoles available.

## Kitchen

Good living starts with good food. The kitchen is fully stocked with quality appliances:
A 30L convection oven reaching up to 300°C, a BALMUDA toaster for perfect toast, a sous vide cooker for tender meat, a pressure cooker, a slow juicer, a blender, a bread maker, a stylish automatic coffee roaster, and a gas takoyaki griddle for group fun.
2 gas burners and a separate IH cooktop. 5 refrigerators/freezers total: 2 on the 5th floor kitchen + 1 chest freezer, plus 1 fridge each on the 4th and 3rd floors.
