## Access

Kobe is where East meets West — and The Headquarters Kobe is right in the thick of it. Just 2 stops from Sannomiya (Kobe's city center) on JR or Hankyu lines, and about a 7-minute walk from JR Maya Station or Hankyu Ojikoen Station. Perfectly located for both everyday life and exploring. Kobe University is also nearby.

The building is situated along the main street of the Suidosuji shopping arcade, under the covered roof. The 1st floor is home to a sock shop — and right next door to the "Suidosuji Koban" (police box), which makes it easy to find on any map app: just search for "水道筋交番."
